Our Mission

Is to foster open dialogue surrounding mental health, encouraging understanding and support for those seeking help. We aim to highlight the unique challenges software developers face, such as burnout and imposter syndrome, while advocating for proactive mental health care within the tech community.

We set out to hear the stories and advice from software developers many whom battle with mental health problems everyday.
